# AWS Well-Architected Framework Principles

This README summarizes the six pillars (principles) of the AWS Well-Architected Framework.

## Pillars

1. **Operational Excellence** – Run and monitor workloads effectively, learn from operations, and continually improve processes and procedures to deliver business value.
2. **Security** – Protect information, systems, and assets while managing risk through strong security practices.
3. **Reliability** – Ensure workloads perform consistently and recover from disruptions, scaling to meet demand and mitigating failures.
4. **Performance Efficiency** – Use computing resources efficiently to meet requirements and maintain efficiency as needs evolve.
5. **Cost Optimization** – Deliver business value at the lowest cost through continuous cost awareness and optimization.
6. **Sustainability** – Reduce environmental impact by improving energy efficiency and minimizing resource usage.
